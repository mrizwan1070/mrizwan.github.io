What we do

Servicing our clients across the world from five locations internationally

We are here to shape better business decision making worldwide. Partnering with the world’s leading investment and consulting companies, as well as the largest global corporate businesses, we enable them to collect real-time information and market insights from industry experts across markets, industries, and regions.

We are building the most trusted and unmatched knowledge-sharing platform in the world to achieve unparalleled customer recognition. We combine highly­ trained client service professionals from across the globe, cutting-edge technology, and a robust compliance framework.

We are recognized as Europe’s fastest-growing expert network in 2021 by the Financial Times, a Best Workplace in Europe for 2021 and in Greece for 2020, 2021 and 2022 by Great Place to Work®.
