Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cashback?

Cashback is a financial benefit offered by Kafcoupons to its registered users, and it means getting back a percentage of the money spent on purchases you got from your favorite stores. This cashback value gets added to your account on Kafcoupons so that you can transfer it to your PayPal account.

When can I request a payment?

When your account's available Cashback amount reaches a minimum of Rs 100, you can request a payment.

Is there anything I need to do to make sure my cashback tracks properly?

If you have any other shopping oriented toolbars or browser add-ons installed (that offer coupon, cashback, etc.), they can interfere with the proper functioning of the cashback Button and therefore earning Cashback. For best results, we recommend removing any other shopping oriented browser add-ons or toolbars.
