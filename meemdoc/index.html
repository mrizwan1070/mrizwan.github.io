Meemdoc, your global health companion with European expertise

Meemdoc is a leading Norwegian telemedicine company that offers a comprehensive range of online healthcare services. Our offerings include GP consultations, prescription services, health tests, and COVID-19 testing. We also provide additional medical services such as issuing sick notes, conducting medical scans like MRI, and facilitating referrals to private hospital care.

Our experienced online doctors at Meemdoc can assist with a wide range of common symptoms and medical issues, just like a Norwegian GP: coughs, colds and flu; chest infections, ear infections, and UTI's; headaches and migraines; erectile dysfunction (ED); mental health issues like stress, anxiety and depression; joint and back pain; asthma, respiratory issues and allergies including Hay Fever; skin issues like eczema and acne; eye problems like conjunctivitis.

What can we do for you

Medical advice

Can’t find a family doctor or walk-in clinic that’s open 24 hours? Maple connects you with a Canadian-licensed family doctor or nurse practitioner online 24/7 so you can ask a doctor for medical advice or get the help you need without having to go to an after-hours clinic.

See more

Prescriptions

Doctors and nurse practitioners can give you a prescription at their discretion, based on your consultation, without needing a walk-in clinic.

See more

Medical notes

Doctors and nurse practitioners can provide sick notes for school or work for short absences, so you can focus on resting instead of sitting in a waiting room.

See more

Lab work

Doctors and nurse practitioners on Maple can order lab work, such as blood tests, if they deem it medically appropriate.

See more

What can't we do

Medical emergencies

Virtual care is not meant for emergencies. In an emergency, please call 911 or go to the ER.

See more

Controlled medications

Our doctors cannot prescribe narcotics.

See more
